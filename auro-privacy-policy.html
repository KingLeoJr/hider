Auro AI Assistant Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how the Auro AI Assistant Chrome extension collects, uses, and shares information.

Information Collection

Auro AI Assistant collects chat history using chrome.storage.local. This data is stored locally on your computer and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information Use

The collected chat history is used to provide context and improve the AI assistant's responses.

Information Sharing

We do not share any collected information with third parties.
